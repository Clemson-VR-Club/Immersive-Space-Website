    Experiences

    From instantly designing a house, to exploring the human brain, to traveling anywhere in the world: virtual reality presents limitless possibilities. The Clemson Immersive Space makes virtual reality experiences available to explore, design, and create in virtual worlds with real-world implications. We've curated a software library that directly meets academic needs on campus -- scroll down to see popular titles below.

    **Note: This list is a work in progress. If you would like to know if a certain experience is available you can visit the Immersive Space during our open hours or email the Immersive Space at vrclub@clemson.edu

    First Experiences

    New to VR? Let's get started.

    Oculus First Contact

    First Contact is a tutorial in which you’ll become acquainted with the VR equipment by learning how to use the controllers, and getting comfortable with being immersed into a virtual world. You’ll play with a multitude of games and toys. From playing with musical instruments to shooting targets, First Contact exposes you to a whole new world.

    Available on:

    Educational Experiences

    Go where you couldn't. See to understand. It's time to learn in a new way.

    3D Organon VR Anatomy

    VR Anatomy “Organon” is a learning tool which allows you to experience the human body up close and personal. You can manipulate bones, muscles, vessels, organs and other anatomical structures in 3D space. Examine structures from all angles, and read or hear anatomical terminology and study definitions.

    Available on:

    Sharecare VR

    Sharecare VR allows you to freely navigate and explore an anatomically accurate 3D model of the human body, its organs, and their natural function. Visualize how your body works. You can customize the anatomy of the body and simulate diseases. Personalize the human body to represent you.

    “The way this game allows you to get up close and personal, is out of this world!” -Immersive Space Newcomer
    Available on:

    Creative Experiences

    Reality shouldn't limit self-expression. Draw, paint, and model to bring your ideas to life.

    Medium

    With Medium you can sculpt, model, and paint in a 3D environment. Use hand gestures and head movements to interact with your sculpts. Quickly and easily create expressive 3D art. Bring whatever is in your imagination to “life”.

    Available on:

    Tilt Brush

    Tilt Brush lets you paint in a 3D space with VR. Use your creativity with 3D brush strokes, stars, light, and even fire! Become the new age Picasso.

    Available on:

    Other Experiences

    Beat Saber

    If you like Guitar Hero, more than likely Beat Saber is the game for you. Beat Saber is a VR rhythm game where the goal is the slash beats (represented by small cubes), using lightsabers, as their coming at you. From the musical stylings of Pop to Rock, this game will have you slicing and dancing.

    “Perfect stress reliever, if you feel like beating something play Beat Saber.” -Immersive Space Gamer
