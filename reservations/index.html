    Reservations

    Making a reservation for access to the Clemson University Immersive Space and a specific piece of equipment is easy and can be done through our online scheduler.

    Tips

    All visitors to the Clemson University Immersive Space need to complete a sign-in form, which is available upon your arrival. This form contains important safety information and should be read carefully.

    Policies and Requirements

    We have implemented the following policies and requirements to ensure that all students, faculty and researches have fair access to the space and equipment. These policies and requirements may be adjusted dependent on demand for the space and equipment.

    • Reservations are thirty (30) minutes long.

    • Reservations must start on the hour (*:00) or half-hour (*:30).

    • Users can attend up to three (3) reservations per week.

    • Reservations can be made up to five (5) days in advanced.

    • Users must provide their Clemson University issued email address (*@clemson.edu or *@g.clemson.edu) when making a reservation.

    In certain circumstances, we may be able to accomodate reservations beyond these requirements. Please reach out by emailing us at vrclub@clemson.edu for additional information.
